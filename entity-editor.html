Background Story

Aric Veyron was born the first son of King Edmund Veyron and Queen Lysandra. From a young age, he showed exceptional intellect and a natural affinity for strategy, often found poring over ancient maps rather than practicing swordplay.

Under his rule, Eldenor entered a new age of prosperity. His vision of a unified continent challenged centuries of tradition, but through careful diplomacy and occasional show of force, he forged alliances that reshaped the known world.

Lore

The Veyron bloodline is said to carry a trace of ancient elven ancestry, granting its heirs sharper intuition and longer lives. Aric was the first of his line to be acknowledged by the Council of Sages as a "Keeper of Balance" — an honor bestowed only upon those who unify rather than conquer.

The golden sigil on his cloak represents the stag of Eldenor — a creature said to appear only in times of great peace or great crisis. Many scholars believe his coronation was foretold in the Prophecy of the Gilded Age.
